# 🌐 Mind Map Maker

Welcome to the Mind Map Maker, bro! This tool is designed to help you visualize your ideas quickly and easily, right in your browser.

## 💾 How Your Data is Saved

This application uses **LocalStorage** to save your work. This means your mind maps are stored directly on your computer's browser, not on a server.

- **🚀 No Login Required**: Start creating immediately without the hassle of signing up.
- **🔒 Privacy**: Your data stays on your device and is never uploaded to the cloud.
- **⚡ Instant Access**: Your maps are ready whenever you return using the same browser.

---

## ⚠️ Important Reminders

Since this is a client-side application, please keep these points in mind:

- **Browser Specific**: Maps created here won't appear if you switch to a different browser or device.
- **Cache Management**: If you clear your browser's site data or cache, your saved maps will be removed.
- **Persistence**: This is a live version hosted on GitHub Pages, ensuring a fast and reliable experience without local setup.

---

## 🚀 Features

- **Intuitive Design**: Clean and minimalist interface for better focus.
- **Auto-Save**: Changes are saved automatically to your local storage.
- **Static & Fast**: Optimized for performance with no backend delays.

**Enjoy building your ideas, bro!** 🚀🔥
